Our Story

Born from
the underground

South of the Sun is a rock band from Yerevan, Armenia - seven musicians driven by the same need to make something raw, powerful, and real.

We pull from metal, modern rock, and post-heavy atmosphere to build songs that feel both intimate and explosive.

Every live set is treated like a statement. Loud, honest, and impossible to fake.
