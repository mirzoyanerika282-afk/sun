Our Story

Born from
the underground

South of the Sun is a rock band from Yerevan, Armenia — seven musicians forged from the same relentless drive to create something raw, powerful, and real.

We draw from the heaviness of metal, the soul of rock, and the atmosphere of post-everything. Our sound is a collision — urgent and expansive, familiar and strange.

We play every show like it's the last one. Because that's the only way worth playing.
