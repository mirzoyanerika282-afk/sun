The story behind South of the Sun.

More Than a Band

South of the Sun began in Yerevan as a shared obsession with heavy music that still leaves room for melody, tension, and atmosphere. The project grew from late rehearsals, underground venues, and the need to make songs that sound as intense live as they feel in the room.

The band mixes modern rock weight, metal energy, and cinematic dynamics. Some songs are built to hit instantly. Others unfold slowly and burn. The goal is always the same: make something honest enough to last.

On stage, South of the Sun leans into impact. The live show is not treated as a copy of the recording. It is the main event. That is why the group keeps building around performance, new releases, and a stronger connection with the audience at every show.
